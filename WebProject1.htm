    What is Digital Marketing?

    Digital Marketing is one of the fastest-growing industries in the world. People of all ages are spending more and more time online, and are turning to the Internet for better services, convenience, and life-enhancing tools.

    Digital marketing refers to the promotion, marketing and advertising of products and services on-line, that is delivered through digital channels such as search engines, websites, social media, e-mail, and mobile apps.
